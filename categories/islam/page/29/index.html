    A Necklace of Fire

    In Surah Lahab, Allah says: وَامْرَأَتُهُ حَمَّالَةَ الْحَطَبِ فِي جِيدِهَا حَبْلٌ مِّن مَّسَدٍ Translation: And his wife, the wood-carrier. Upon her neck is a twisted rope of Masad (palm fibre). Abu Lahab and his wife were some of the most severe opponents to the Prophet (صلي الله عليه وسلم). They fully understood his message and recognized him as a Prophet, and still they opposed him, until the end of their days.
