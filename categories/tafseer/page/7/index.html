    In Surah Lahab, Allah says: وَامْرَأَتُهُ حَمَّالَةَ الْحَطَبِ فِي جِيدِهَا حَبْلٌ مِّن مَّسَدٍ Translation: And his wife, the wood-carrier. Upon her neck is a twisted rope of Masad (palm fibre).
